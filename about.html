Meet the team of Next

The team supporting the University of Cincinnati isn't simply looking for a paycheck, they're looking to impact student's lives and help students meaningful connections on campus. Our team is poised and ready to support UC's growth and strategic objectives.


We are excited about the additions we have added to the team with our Executive Director of Hospitality, Director of Quality Assurance, Director of Technology, Sustainability Coordinator, and COOP and Community Liason. We are here to serve the University of Cincinnati!

Saxbys

Our collaboration with Saxbys, provides a great opportunity for students to gain valuable skills and work experience, while serving great coffee and food. Through Saxbys Experiential Learning Platform, the café will be completely run by undergraduates learning hands-on lessons in team development, financial management, and community outreach.


The café will be managed by a dedicated, full-time student café executive officer (SCEO), who receives ongoing mentorship and training, and earns wages and potentially academic credits over the course of a six-month term.
