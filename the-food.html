Bearcats Culinary Group

Throughout our partnership, Bearcats Culinary Groups’ goal has always been to deliver a high-quality residential dining program that provides authentic flavors, numerous opportunities for customization, healthy options, and trending student favorites. As part of our revitalized residential vision, our residential dining halls will transform into three distinctive dining destinations with its own culinary focus.

With investment in culinary staff and resources, we look to continue our engagement with our students. We are motivated to continue building and innovating to become a top-tier program through new culinary talents, our students’ feedback, the addition of local partners, and inclusion of Cincinnati food trends and beyond.

Athenian Grill

Our Athenian Grill partner delivers a multitude of Mediterranean inspired dishes that are sure to satisfy your cravings while also providing plenty of nutrients to fuel your busy day. Our partnership allows us to bring high quality ethnic foods to On the Green!

Choolah

Choolah is our Indian partner that specializes in serving traditional Indian cuisine to food lovers. With its authentic and mouthwatering dishes, Choolah offers a unique dining experience that is hard to forget. With a commitment to using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients, Choola is the perfect partner to bring authentic Indian flavors to campus.

The 86 Coffee Bar

Making a positive impact on the community, The 86 Coffee Bar is an exciting addition to the Bearcats Culinary Group retail program. Starting January 2024, The 86 Coffee Bar will replace Starbucks and will begin serving our Bearcats. This non-profit, mission-minded, student volunteer operated coffee shop will provide a great place for students to hang out for coffee, creativity, and community.

Clifton Catering

Clifton Catering offers exciting, personalized menus that satisfy every appetite and enhance every event. We use the freshest seasonal ingredients to create delicious and innovative food offerings. Our catering menus are comprehensive yet flexible, including options for vegetarian, vegan, local, and sustainable dining preferences, as well as addressing special dietary needs. We have specifically designed menus to fit every occasion.
