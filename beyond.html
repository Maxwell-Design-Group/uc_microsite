The Future of Bearcats Culinary Group

Supporting the growth of the university through innovative solutions.

Our Commitment

The Bearcats Culinary Group is committed to proactively measuring and monitoring our performance around Student Experience, Quality Assurance, All Access meal plan usage, Local and Sustainable purchases, Strategic Planning and Administrative Engagement.

Continuing the Momentum Together

We are grateful for our long-standing partnership with UC and for the opportunity to share an aligned vision for creating a top-tier hospitality program. To this day, we have evolved and are prepared with new, experienced leadership to take our program to the next level, enhancing the student experience to match the University’s reputation.

As the University continues to grow as a beacon of change, an academic innovator, and a model for innovative, forward thinking, we will grow right along with you, making the atmosphere at UC a community that no one can replicate. We are excited to continue our partnership and contribution to UC’s upward trajectory as your aligned, trusted partner who sees limitless potential in creating a stunningly Cincy showcase program.
