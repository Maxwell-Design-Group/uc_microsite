Our Initiatives

Contributing to the success and growth of the university by supporting key initiatives.

Health & Wellness

Nutrislice

Nutrislice enables us to make your food program safer and provide real time information. Our new partnership with Spotlight allows the system to integrate with our menu management system, hence anabling the accuracy of our eduction around nutritionals, allergens, and ingredients.

CampusDish

Bearcats will have access to personalized health and wellness options with clear iconography, including important allergen information through our nutrition management program on their desktop and mobile device with our CampusDish website and app.

"I am excited to be a part of the Bearcat community and drive the health and wellness program here at University of Cincinnati. Nutrition plays a vital role in the lives and success of our students. From food allergies and intolerances, dietary preferences, and personal health goals, working at Bearcats Culinary Group allows me to guide students with their nutrition as they enter a new chapter in their lives."

Samantha Krutka

Director of Dietetics and Wellness

Recycling Education

Financial Transparency

Transparency and accuracy of our records is important to the University of Cincinnati. We are committed to conducting all business with the utmost integrity, transparency, and according to the highest ethical standards. That is why we are building out a client facing operational and financial dashboard.

Creating a Connected
Campus Community

We connect with your campus community on their terms through personalized communication by leveraging traditional, digital, and social strategies. Our marketing program creates opportunities to spotlight events showcasing health and wellness and overall well-being, fun events, promotions, and supports the overall growth of the meal plan program. Our goal is to develop a connected campus ecosystem where your students learn to thrive, both on their own and as a community.
