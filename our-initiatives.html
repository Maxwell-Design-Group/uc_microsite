Health & Wellness

Nutrislice enables us to make your food program safer and provide real time information. Our new partnership with Spotlight allows the system to integrate with our menu management system, hence anabling the accuracy of our eduction around nutritionals, allergens, and ingredients.

Recycling Education

Oscar provides recycling education at the source. Instead of tracking plastic bags within dumpsters, cleaning oceans, or trying ot undo contamination levels at recycling centers, Oscar sort nudges students and faculty at the source.
